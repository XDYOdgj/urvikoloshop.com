EUSTUMA LED Light Base - Stylish Display Solution for Glass & Crystal

The EUSTUMA LED Light Base is more than just a display stand; it's a statement piece that blends style with functionality. Its non-slip base is equipped with 1 mm protrusions to keep your glass, crystal, or spherical decor items securely in place, eliminating the risk of accidental movement. With a simple touch switch, you can effortlessly cycle through 6 LED lighting modes, including slow and fast color transitions, as well as constant monochrome lighting in white, red, blue, and green. This flexibility ensures the stand adapts to your mood or any event from daily use to festive gatherings. The compact design makes it ideal for smaller decor items, while the vibrant color options bring a dynamic and eye-catching appeal to any room. Whether in your home or office, this display stand enhances the visual charm of your space.

  • Non-slip base with 1 mm protrusions for secure placement
  • Touch switch with 6 LED modes: slow color change, fast color change, constant white, red, blue, and green
  • Adjustable height for versatile use with various items
  • Built-in USB compatibility and 3 AAA batteries (not included) for easy power options
  • Stylish and modern design suitable for home and office decor
