JUXYES Clear Sphere Display Stands

Elegant & Functional Decor for Your Collection

These 12 clear sphere display stands are designed to elevate your decor with their durable, transparent construction. Perfect for showcasing a variety of small decorative items like golf balls, marbles, crystal balls, and more, they offer a clean and modern way to display your favorite pieces.

Their sleek design brings a touch of sophistication to any space, making them ideal for home, office, or retail environments. With smooth edges and a secure ring that holds spheres firmly in place, these stands are both functional and stylish, ensuring your collection looks impeccable.

  • Sturdy Clear Plastic Construction: Built to last, these stands provide reliable support for any decorative spheres.
