Handmade Hanging Sleigh Bells

These beautifully crafted sleigh bells are the perfect way to add a touch of traditional elegance to your front door or shop entrance. Each bell is made of polished brass, giving them a timeless, vintage look that complements both seasonal and everyday decor.

The sturdy green leather strap ensures these bells remain securely in place, creating a gentle, melodic jingle every time someone enters or exits your home or business. Whether you're looking for festive door decor that brings a sense of joy and nostalgia or a functional shopkeepers bell to alert customers, these sleigh bells offer both style and purpose.

Designed for easy installation, the mini lace loop on each bell allows for quick and hassle-free setup. These bells can be used year-round, making them a versatile addition to your home's decor or a charming feature in your holiday display.

With a blend of quality craftsmanship and classic design, these door bell decorations are ideal for anyone who appreciates the beauty of everyday accessories and wants to enhance their space with a heartfelt, functional touch.
