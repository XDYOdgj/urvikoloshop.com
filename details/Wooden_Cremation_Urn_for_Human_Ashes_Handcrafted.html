Handcrafted Wooden Cremation Urn

This elegant wooden urn is a thoughtful and heartfelt tribute to a loved one. Made from high-quality solid mango wood, it beautifully showcases the natural grain and texture, offering a unique and organic finish that honors the memory of the departed.

  • Constructed from solid mango wood with a rich, natural grain
  • Secure aluminum threaded lid ensures a safe and dependable closure
  • Capable of holding up to 150 lbs of ashes with ease

Why Choose This Urn?

More than just a container, this urn is a meaningful keepsake that blends traditional craftsmanship with a modern, minimalist aesthetic. Its durable construction ensures longevity, while the thoughtful details make it a beautiful addition to any room or memorial setting.

Whether you're creating a family memorial, keeping a personal remembrance, or adding a meaningful piece to your home decor, this urn offers a dignified and graceful way to honor your loved one's life.

Product Description

This handcrafted wooden cremation urn is designed with both function and elegance in mind.
